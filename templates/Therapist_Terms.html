TherapyTalk - Terms and Conditions for Therapists

Welcome to TherapyTalk. By registering as a therapist on our platform, you agree to abide by the following terms and conditions.

1. Accuracy of Information

  • Therapists must provide accurate and truthful details during registration. Providing false or misleading information may lead to legal consequences and immediate account termination.
  • All therapists must submit valid certifications and credentials for verification.
  • TherapyTalk reserves the right to verify submitted documents at any time.

2. Platform Charge and Payment Structure

  • TherapyTalk deducts 20% of the total session fee as a platform charge. Example: If your session fee is ₹300, TherapyTalk will deduct ₹60, and you will receive the remaining ₹240.
  • Payments are transferred based on your selected payment schedule.
  • Patients pay in advance, and fees are collected in the admin account before being transferred to you.
  • Payment disputes must be reported within 7 days for resolution.

3. Cancellations and No-Response Policy

  • If you cancel a session, a penalty of ₹50 per session will be deducted. Frequent no-shows may lead to account suspension.
  • If a patient is assigned to you and you fail to respond or conduct the scheduled sessions, the admin reserves the right to terminate or block your account.
  • Frequent cancellations or no-shows may lead to temporary or permanent suspension from the platform.

4. Code of Conduct

  • Therapists must maintain a high level of professionalism and ethical behavior. Any misconduct may result in immediate termination.
  • Any form of misconduct, harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior will lead to immediate account termination.
  • All sessions must be conducted exclusively through Google Meet or Zoom. The admin has the right to join any session at any time for monitoring and quality assurance purposes.
  • External communication with patients for session purposes is strictly prohibited.

5. Fee Stability

  • Session fees cannot be changed for six months. Any modifications require prior approval.
